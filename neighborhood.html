Description

The community was named for grape vineyards near the original town site. Nestled between Orem City and Utah Lake, Vineyard has the feel of a rural community but is close to many city amenities. With beautiful lakefront views of Utah Lake to the west and Mt. Timpanogos to the east, Vineyard has a unique beauty and charm all its own!



Vineyard MAYrathon!

Vineyard City Recreation account. Join us during the month of May for the Vineyard "MAYrathon"! Run, bike, or walk 26.2 miles during the month to win an event t-shirt and medal!



Welcome to Vineyard, Utah, where the majestic mountains meet the serene Utah Lake, creating a picturesque backdrop in one of the best neighborhoods in Utah! Nestled in this scenic paradise, you'll find the Vineyard Golf Course, a golfer's dream come true. Tee off against the stunning mountain vistas, and let the crisp mountain air and lush green fairways transport you to golfing heaven.
